Abstract Data Type Specification

The queue follows the First-In-First-Out (FIFO) principle, a fundamental concept in computer science analogous to real-world queues (Cormen, Leiserson, Rivest, & Stein, 2022). This implementation uses a dynamic array with amortized O(1) complexity for both enqueue and dequeue operations.

The circular buffer optimization prevents memory leaks from abandoned indices at the front of the array, maintaining O(1) time complexity without the O(n) reallocation penalty of shift-based implementations (Sedgewick & Wayne, 2011).
